Before the Draft: A Look at the 2012-13 Season

Context and key storylines to set the stage for the 2013 NHL Draft analysis.

Shortened Season

The 2012-13 NHL season was shortened to 48 games due to a lockout. This affected team performance, player statistics, and draft scouting evaluations.

NHL Player Positions

Forward (Center, Left Wing, Right Wing): Responsible for scoring and offensive play.
Defenseman: Protects the goal, blocks shots, and supports offensive plays.
Goaltender: Guards the net and prevents the opposing team from scoring.
